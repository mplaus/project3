UNC Employee Salary vs. Tuition rates

This graph compares how the salaries of faculty members, upper level administrators, and the price of tuition at UNC chapel Hill has changed over time.

The data, drawn from the university's HR department, includes the salary of the athletic director, average faculty salary and median faculty salary, and in-state and out of state tuition going back to the year 2000.

The select series dropdown bar allows you to view the the whole chart, the change between faculty salary and tuition, or simply the change in tuition.
